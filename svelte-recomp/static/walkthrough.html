🏠 Today

Your daily home screen — quick logging, streaks, readiness.

🔥 Logging streak

Shows how many consecutive days you've logged anything (weight, food, a workout set, or the quick-log card). Missing a single day doesn't reset it to zero — there's a 1-day "shield" built in, so real life doesn't wreck your motivation.

Daily Readiness score New

Tap "Log today" to enter last night's sleep hours, sleep quality (1-5), and optionally resting heart rate / HRV if you track those. Get a 0-100 score (Low/Fair/Good/Great) telling you whether to push hard today or take it easier — the same idea behind Whoop/Oura's Recovery score, done with numbers you type in instead of a wearable.

Quick Log

Weight, calories, steps — one tap to save. Shows a green "Saved ✓" or the real error text if something goes wrong (never fails silently).

Evening Checklist

Swipe left on any item to reveal a big Delete button. Tap the checkbox to mark done. Add your own items at the bottom.

kg Goal Editable

Tap the "kg Goal" number on the scorecard row to edit your target weight — it syncs across every device instantly.

⏰ Alarms

Real push notifications, synced across every device you're logged into.

Adding an alarm

Tap + Add Alarm, set a title, optional message, time, and which days it repeats on.

Editing & deleting

Tap an alarm card to edit it. Swipe left to reveal a big Delete button — no confirmation popup needed (the swipe itself is the "are you sure").

⚠️ Silent mode limitation

Web notifications cannot override your phone's silent switch — this is an intentional OS security restriction from Apple/Google, not something any website can bypass (otherwise any site could blast sound at you against your will). Only your native Clock/Alarm app has that special permission.

What you can do: go to your phone's notification settings for RecompOS and allow it to override Do Not Disturb / bypass silent mode manually. On Android: Settings → Apps → RecompOS → Notifications → enable "Override Do Not Disturb". On iPhone, PWA notifications follow Safari's notification permissions and unfortunately have no such override — iOS is stricter here.

🥗 Food

Real macro tracking, not just a calorie number.

Nutrition — today's totals

Tracks kcal, protein, carbs, and fat per entry (not just calories). Shows a protein target bar based on ~2g per kg of your current bodyweight (pulled live from your weigh-ins).

📷 Barcode scanner New

Tap Scan barcode to open your camera and scan the barcode on any packaged food. It looks up the product in the free Open Food Facts database (3M+ products) and fills in the macros for you.

What it can't do: it only reads barcodes — it can't look at a photo of a home-cooked meal and guess the calories. That would need a different (paid) image-recognition service, which isn't built yet.

Values come back per 100g — adjust the numbers for your actual portion size before saving.

Swipe to delete

Swipe left on any logged food to remove it.

Batch Cook

10 chicken curry recipes with full ingredient lists and stovetop/Instant Pot instructions. Assign a recipe to each day of the week in "This Week's Plan" — syncs across devices.

📈 Track

Weight trend, steps, water, and visual progress.

Weight chart

Log your weight and watch the trend line build over time. Shows kg lost since your first entry and weeks-to-goal at your current rate of loss.

📸 Progress photos New

Take or upload front/side/back photos, tagged by date. Pick any two dates and drag the slider to compare them side-by-side — often shows visible recomposition progress well before the scale does. Photos are private to your account only.

Steps & Water

Log daily steps. Tap water droplets to track glasses (8-glass daily goal).

🏋 Gym

Your badminton-aware weekly training plan, fully editable, with real progress tracking.

Muscle Recovery grid New

Shows every major muscle group as Fresh (<24h since last trained), Recovering (24-48h), or Ready (>48h) — based on what you've actually logged, so you always know what's safe to hit hard today.

Training Load Balance New

Compares your last 7 days of training volume to your last 28 days. If the ratio spikes too high, it's a well-documented injury-risk signal (the same "acute:chronic workload ratio" sports scientists use) — appears once you have a few weeks of logged history.

Weekly schedule

Tap the ✎ on any day to change its label, note, or which session it maps to. Your badminton nights (Wed/Fri 7-9pm) are already built in as your cardio days.

Logging a session

Open a session, then for each exercise:

  • Tap Log sets to enter weight × reps per set (auto-prefilled from last time)
  • Tap 🏋 Plates for a plate-by-plate breakdown of what to load on the bar
  • Tap 📈 Warm-up for a suggested warm-up ladder (40/50/60/75/90% of your work weight)
  • Tap ⏱ Rest to start a countdown timer with a vibration + chime when it's done
  • Tap Save log when you're done with that exercise

PRs & progress charts

Once you've logged an exercise twice, you'll see a 🏆 PR badge (best set ever, by estimated 1-rep-max) and a "Progress" chart showing your strength trend over time.

Auto-progression suggestions

After logging a set, get a plain-English suggestion: increase the weight (you hit the top of your rep range), hold steady and push for more reps, or deload 10% (you missed reps badly last time).

Exercise videos & photos

Every exercise has a real offline photo (works with zero signal) and a "Watch full video" button for a proper YouTube tutorial when you're online.

🎵 Amazon Music New

Tap Open Amazon Music at the top of the Gym page to jump straight into your Amazon Music app for a workout playlist. Limitation: Amazon doesn't offer any way for other apps to control playback in-app (Spotify does, Amazon doesn't) — so this is a one-way handoff, not an embedded player. You'll leave RecompOS and come back manually.

🧮 Plan

Body composition estimates and goal scenarios.

Navy method body fat %

Enter height, neck, and waist (+ hip if female) for a body-fat estimate using the well-established U.S. Navy tape-measure method.

📷 Photo estimate (Gemini Vision)

Upload a front/side photo for an AI-based body-fat estimate. This just got fixed this session (it was silently failing due to a technical bug) — try it again if it didn't work before.

Goal scenarios

See your estimated Lean Body Mass at different target body-fat percentages (Lean/Fit/Healthy), calculated from your latest logged weight.

📲 Installing as an app

iPhone

Open the app in Safari → tap the Share icon → Add to Home Screen.

Android

Open the app in Chrome → tap the ⋮ menu → Install app (or you'll see an automatic install prompt).

Once installed, most of the app works fully offline — exercise photos, your logged data, and the gym plan are all cached on your device and sync automatically the next time you're on wifi or data.
